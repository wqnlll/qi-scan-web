Why Use AI to Build Websites?

AI has dramatically lowered the barrier to web development. Tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Cursor can generate complete websites in minutes. Whether you're building a portfolio, blog, or business site, AI can handle the heavy lifting.

Step 2: Write Your Prompt

The key to getting great results is writing clear, specific prompts. Here's an example:

Create a modern, responsive landing page for a tech consulting company. Include:
- A hero section with headline and CTA button
- A features section with 3 cards
- A testimonials section
- A contact form
- A footer with social links
Use a clean, professional design with blue and white colors.

Step 3: Refine and Customize

Once the AI generates your initial code, you can iterate by asking for changes:

  1. Change the color scheme to match your brand
  2. Add or remove sections as needed
  3. Optimize for SEO with proper meta tags
  4. Improve accessibility with ARIA labels
