Using AI Image Generators for Your Website

Create stunning visuals for your website using AI image generation tools like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion �?no designer needed.

By Qi Scan Team · Jun 5, 2026 · 7 min read · AI Tools

Why AI Images Matter for Your Website

Visual content increases engagement by up to 80%. But hiring a designer for every image is expensive and slow. AI image generators solve both problems �?they're fast, affordable, and the quality has reached a point where most visitors can't tell the difference.

Top 3 AI Image Generators in 2026

1. Midjourney v7 (Best Quality)

Midjourney remains the gold standard for artistic quality. Its latest v7 model produces photorealistic images with incredible detail. Perfect for hero images, blog illustrations, and social media graphics.

  • Strengths: Photorealism, artistic style, consistency
  • Cost: $10/month starter plan
  • Best for: Professional websites, marketing materials

2. DALL-E 3 (Best Integration)

Built into ChatGPT Plus, DALL-E 3 follows instructions precisely and integrates seamlessly with your existing workflow. Great for generating images that match specific brand guidelines.

  • Strengths: Text accuracy, instruction following, ChatGPT integration
  • Cost: Included in ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
  • Best for: Quick iterations, branded content

3. Stable Diffusion XL (Best for Control)

Open-source and self-hostable, SDXL gives you complete control over the generation process. Use with Automatic1111 or ComfyUI for advanced workflows like inpainting, outpainting, and style transfer.

  • Strengths: Free, customizable, local deployment
  • Cost: Free (requires GPU)
  • Best for: Developers, advanced users

Prompt Engineering for Web Images

The key to great AI images is writing effective prompts. Here's a formula that works consistently:

# Image prompt formula:
[Subject] + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Composition] + [Details]

# Examples:
"A modern SaaS dashboard on a laptop, clean minimalist style, 
soft natural lighting, centered composition, blue and white 
color palette, 4k resolution"

"Team of diverse professionals collaborating in a bright office, 
photorealistic style, golden hour lighting, shallow depth of field, 
warm tones"

Optimizing Images for Web

AI-generated images are often large files. Here's how to optimize them:

  1. Resize: Match your display dimensions (don't upload 4000px wide images for a 400px slot)
  2. Convert: Use WebP format for 30% smaller file sizes
  3. Compress: Use TinyPNG or Squoosh for lossless compression
  4. Lazy load: Add loading="lazy" to all images below the fold

Common Use Cases

  • Hero backgrounds: Full-width abstract or lifestyle images
  • Blog illustrations: Custom graphics for each article
  • Social media: Platform-specific sized images
  • Product photos: AI-generated lifestyle shots for e-commerce
  • Icons and graphics: Custom icon sets matching your brand

Legal Considerations

Most AI-generated images are free to use commercially, but always check the terms of service for your chosen tool. Midjourney and DALL-E allow commercial use on paid plans. Stable Diffusion is completely open-source.

Conclusion

AI image generators have democratized professional visual design. Whether you're building a personal blog or a business website, you can create stunning imagery that rivals custom-designed visuals �?at a fraction of the cost and time.
