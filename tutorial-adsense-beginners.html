Google AdSense for Beginners: Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about applying for Google AdSense, placing ads on your website, and maximizing your revenue from day one.

By Qi Scan Team · Jun 12, 2026 · 8 min read · Monetization

What is Google AdSense?

Google AdSense is a free advertising program that allows website owners to earn money by displaying targeted ads on their websites. Google matches relevant ads to your content, and you get paid when visitors click on those ads or view them (CPM model).

In 2026, AdSense remains one of the most reliable ways to monetize a website �?especially for content-driven sites like blogs, tutorials, and niche resource pages.

Step 1: Meet the Eligibility Requirements

Before applying, make sure your website meets these criteria:

  • Original content: At least 15-20 high-quality articles or pages
  • Website age: Ideally 3+ months old (though not strictly required)
  • Traffic: Some organic traffic helps, but not mandatory
  • Navigate structure: Clear menu, About page, Contact page
  • No policy violations: No copyrighted content, adult material, or dangerous content

Step 2: Apply for AdSense

Visit adsense.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click "Start earning" and enter your website URL. Google will review your site within a few days to a couple of weeks.

Step 3: Place Ads Strategically

Where you place ads matters enormously for both revenue and user experience. Here are the best positions:

  1. Header banner (728x90): Above the fold, highest visibility
  2. In-content native ads: Between paragraphs, feels natural
  3. Sidebar rectangle (300x250): Classic placement, good CTR
  4. Footer sticky banner: Mobile-friendly, doesn't block content
  5. After the first heading: Captures attention early

Step 4: Optimize for Maximum Revenue

Here are proven strategies to increase your AdSense earnings:

  • Focus on high-CPM niches: Finance, tech, and business topics pay more per click
  • Increase page views: More pages = more ad impressions
  • Improve SEO: Organic traffic converts better than social traffic
  • Test ad sizes: Larger ads (300x600, 336x280) typically earn more
  • Use Auto Ads wisely: Let Google's AI place ads, but review placement regularly

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Placing too many ads (over 3 ad units per page hurts UX)
  • Accidentally clicking your own ads (can get your account banned)
  • Ignoring mobile optimization (50%+ of traffic is mobile)
  • Publishing low-quality content just for ad revenue

Conclusion

Google AdSense is a powerful monetization tool when used correctly. Focus on creating valuable content, placing ads strategically, and continuously optimizing. Most beginners see $50-200/month within the first 6 months with consistent effort.
